> **Version:** 2.0 | **Last Updated:** February 2026 | **Classification:** Partner & Sales Use

---

## Tier Overview

Rainbow offers five subscription tiers designed to serve every segment from individual freelancers to large-scale multi-company ecosystems.

| Tier | Target Audience | Positioning |
|---|---|---|
| **Essential** | Individuals, freelancers, trial users | Free entry point to the Rainbow ecosystem |
| **Business** | SMBs, departments, growing teams | Full collaboration with conferencing and PBX |
| **Enterprise** | Mid-to-large organizations | Complete UCaaS with AI, analytics, and CRM |
| **Connect** | ISVs, developers, system integrators | CPaaS — embeddable APIs and SDKs |
| **Hub** | Channel partners, multi-company ecosystems | Multi-tenant federation and management |

## Quick Decision Matrix

**Choose Essential if:** Testing Rainbow, personal use, or small teams under 6 who only need messaging and basic calls.

**Choose Business if:** The organization needs conferencing, PBX integration, and standard collaboration for day-to-day teamwork.

**Choose Enterprise if:** The organization requires advanced telephony (IVR, ACD), CRM connectors, AI features, compliance recording, or data sovereignty via Rainbow Edge.

**Choose Connect if:** A software vendor or integrator needs to embed Rainbow communication features directly into their own application.

**Choose Hub if:** A channel partner or ecosystem orchestrator needs to manage and federate multiple companies from a single administration point.
