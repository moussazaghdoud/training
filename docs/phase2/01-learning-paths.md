# Rainbow Training Platform — Learning Paths by Persona

> Phase 2 Deliverable | Curriculum Design & Learning Path Architecture

---

## OVERVIEW

Each persona follows a **4-level progressive path**:

| Level | Label | Purpose | Typical Duration |
|---|---|---|---|
| L1 | **Foundation** | Core concepts, platform orientation, basic workflows | 2-4 hours |
| L2 | **Practitioner** | Day-to-day proficiency, intermediate features, real scenarios | 4-6 hours |
| L3 | **Expert** | Advanced features, strategic application, complex use cases | 4-8 hours |
| L4 | **Champion** | Leadership, mentoring, business transformation, certification | 2-4 hours |

**Total per track:** 12-22 hours of structured content
